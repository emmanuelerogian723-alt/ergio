🚀Getting Started

ERGIO is your all-in-one AI business platform. It builds professional websites, crawls the internet to find paying clients, writes outreach messages, accepts payments, and manages your entire business — automatically.

🏗️Build a Website 🔍Find Clients 💳Get Paid 📊Dashboard

Quick Start (3 Steps)

1

Create Your Free Account

Go to ergio.vercel.app/auth/signup.html and sign up with your email. It's free forever — no credit card needed.

2

Describe Your Business

Type what your business does in the prompt box. Example: "A luxury hair salon in Lagos called GlowUp Beauty". ERGIO's AI builds your website in under 60 seconds.

3

Launch & Find Clients

Click Deploy to publish your website, then go to the Leads tab in your dashboard to find people who need your service. ERGIO gives you their names, phone numbers, and intent scores.

💡 Pro Tip

The more detail you include in your prompt, the better your website will be. Mention your business name, type, location, services, and the vibe you want.

🏗️Building Your Website

ERGIO uses a Lovable-style split-screen builder. You describe your business on the left, and the live preview appears on the right in real-time.

What Happens When You Build

  • Step 1 — Plan: AI analyzes your prompt and creates a business plan (name, tagline, services, target market, SEO keywords, design style)
  • Step 2 — Brand: AI creates your brand identity (logo, colors, typography, voice, unique selling point)
  • Step 3 — Images: ERGIO searches Pixabay and Unsplash for real, high-quality photos that match your business
  • Step 4 — Content: AI writes professional copy (hero headline, about section, service descriptions, testimonials, FAQ)
  • Step 5 — Website: ERGIO assembles everything into a stunning, responsive HTML website with animations
  • Step 6 — SEO: Meta tags, Open Graph tags, sitemap, and keyword optimization are automatically added

Prompt Tips for Best Results

✨ Prompt Formula

[adjective] [business type] in [city] called [business name]

  • Good: A modern restaurant in Lagos called Kings Table
  • Better: A luxury hair salon in Lekki, Lagos called GlowUp Beauty. We offer hair styling, braids, and spa treatments. Target wealthy women aged 25-45.
  • Best: A tech startup in Abuja called PayNaija. We're a mobile payment app for small businesses. Our target market is market traders and SMEs. The vibe should be modern and trustworthy.

🔍Finding Paying Clients

This is ERGIO's superpower. While other website builders stop at building your site, ERGIO actively crawls the internet to find people who need your service.

The 4 Client Acquisition Engines

📍

1. Local Discovery

Creates SEO-optimized pages and submits your business to online directories. Gets you found on Google.

🎯

2. Demand Matching

Scans 70+ search engines for people asking about your service. Scrapes their contact info and AI-scores their intent.

📧

3. AI Outreach

Writes personalized cold emails and WhatsApp messages for each lead. Sends them via Resend.

🔄

4. Repeat Clients

Automated WhatsApp follow-ups to past clients to bring them back. Set it once, it runs forever.

How to Use the Lead Scanner

1

Go to Dashboard → Leads

Log in and navigate to the Leads tab in your dashboard.

2

Enter Your Business Type & City

Example: Business type = "hair salon", City = "Lagos". Click "Scan for Leads".

3

Review Qualified Leads

ERGIO returns leads with: name, email, phone number, location, intent score (0-100), and why they need your service. Sort by score to find the hottest leads.

4

Generate Outreach Messages

Click "Generate Outreach" on any lead. ERGIO writes a personalized email and WhatsApp message. Review, edit if needed, and send.

⚡ API Quick Start

POST to /api/leads with {"businessType":"hair salon","city":"Lagos","limit":8}

📧AI Outreach

ERGIO writes personalized cold outreach messages for each lead using AI. It generates three formats:

  • Email: Professional cold email with subject line and body
  • WhatsApp: Short, friendly WhatsApp message (most effective in Nigeria)
  • SMS: Ultra-short SMS version for text marketing

How to Generate Outreach

  • From the Leads tab, select one or more leads
  • Click "Generate Outreach Messages"
  • ERGIO writes a unique message for each lead based on their profile
  • Copy the message and send it via WhatsApp, email, or SMS
💡 Nigeria Tip

WhatsApp is king in Nigeria. Always send the WhatsApp version first — response rates are 5-10x higher than email.

💳Accepting Payments

ERGIO integrates with Paystack — Nigeria's most trusted payment processor. Accept cards, bank transfers, USSD, and mobile money.

Payment Methods Supported

  • 💳 Cards: Visa, Mastercard, Verve
  • 🏦 Bank Transfer: All major Nigerian banks
  • 📱 USSD: *737#, *904#, etc.
  • 💰 Mobile Money: Paga, OPay, etc.

How to Accept a Payment

  • Go to Dashboard → Payments
  • Enter the amount, client email, and description
  • Click "Generate Payment Link"
  • Share the Paystack checkout URL with your client
  • Payment is confirmed automatically via webhook
⚡ API Quick Start

POST to /api/payments with {"amount":15000,"email":"client@example.com"}

📅Booking Management

🔌Plugins & MCP

ERGIO supports built-in plugins and MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers that let the AI use external tools during website generation.

Built-in Plugins

  • 🔍 Web Search: Search the web for information during builds
  • 🌐 HTTP Fetch: Fetch any URL for content or API data
  • 🕷️ Web Scraper: Scrape web pages for content
  • 🎨 Image AI: Generate custom AI images
  • 🤖 AI Text: Generate text content with AI
  • 📧 Email: Send emails via Resend
  • 🎭 Figma Import: Import designs from Figma
  • 🐙 GitHub: Deploy to GitHub repositories

MCP Servers

MCP (Model Context Protocol) lets ERGIO connect to external tools. Available MCP integrations:

  • Figma MCP: Import Figma designs and use them as design references
  • GitHub MCP: Deploy code to GitHub repos automatically
  • Supabase MCP: Run database queries
  • Slack MCP: Send messages to Slack channels
  • Notion MCP: Access Notion pages
  • Custom MCP: Connect any MCP-compatible server
💡 How to Use

In the builder, click the 🔌 Plugins or ⚡ MCP button in the chat input to open the quick picker. Toggle tools on/off or connect MCP servers.

⚙️API Reference

ERGIO has a full REST API with 25+ endpoints. All endpoints are at https://ergio.vercel.app/api/[endpoint].

Core Endpoints

POST /api/generate     — Build a website (SSE stream)
POST /api/refine       — Edit website via chat (SSE stream)
POST /api/leads        — Find qualified leads
POST /api/outreach     — Generate outreach messages
POST /api/payments     — Initialize Paystack payment
GET  /api/payments     — Verify payment status
POST /api/bookings     — Create a booking
GET  /api/bookings     — List bookings
POST /api/invoices     — Generate invoice
POST /api/expenses     — Log expense
POST /api/crm          — Add/manage clients
POST /api/assistant    — AI business advisor chat
POST /api/mcp          — MCP tool calls
GET  /api/engines      — Engine status
GET  /api/analytics    — Business analytics

Example: Build a Website

curl -X POST https://ergio.vercel.app/api/generate \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"prompt":"A modern restaurant in Lagos called Kings Table"}'

Example: Find Leads

curl -X POST https://ergio.vercel.app/api/leads \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"businessType":"hair salon","city":"Lagos","limit":8}'

❓FAQ

Is ERGIO free?

Yes! ERGIO has a free forever plan that includes website building, lead scanning, and basic features. Premium features (unlimited leads, custom domains, advanced analytics) are available on paid plans.

How fast does ERGIO build a website?

Most websites are generated in 10-30 seconds. The AI plans your business, creates a brand identity, searches for real photos, writes professional copy, and assembles the HTML — all automatically.

Do I need to know how to code?

No! ERGIO is designed for non-technical entrepreneurs. Just describe your business in plain English and ERGIO does everything else. You can edit your website by clicking elements or typing chat commands.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Deploy to GitHub Pages or Netlify and connect your custom domain. Or use ERGIO's free hosting at ergio.vercel.app/s/your-business.

How does ERGIO find clients?

ERGIO scans 70+ search engines for people asking about your service, scrapes their contact information, and uses AI to score their intent. It also generates personalized outreach messages for each lead.

What payment methods are supported?

ERGIO uses Paystack, which supports all Nigerian payment methods: debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, USSD, and mobile money (Paga, OPay).

Can I use ERGIO outside Nigeria?

Yes! ERGIO works globally, but it's optimized for the African market with Nigerian Naira pricing, Paystack payments, Nigerian city data, and local business knowledge.

How do I get help?

Click the "Guide" button in the navigation bar to replay the interactive tour at any time. You can also chat with Ergie (the AI assistant) directly in the builder for help with anything.

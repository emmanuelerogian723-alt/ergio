🚀 Getting Started — Build Your First Website

ERGIO builds complete, professional websites from a single text prompt. No coding needed.

Step 1: Describe Your Business

Go to ergio.vercel.app and type what your business does in the prompt box.

A modern restaurant in Lagos serving Nigerian fusion cuisine with online ordering

Be specific — include your city, what you do, and any special features. The AI uses this to generate your entire website.

Step 2: Watch the AI Build

ERGIO's AI goes through 8 steps automatically:

  1. 🧠 Analyzes your business vision and creates a plan
  2. 🎨 Generates brand identity (logo, colors, tagline)
  3. 📸 Searches for real photos (Pixabay, Unsplash)
  4. ✍️ Writes premium website copy (Nigerian context)
  5. 🏗️ Builds the HTML website with animations
  6. 🔍 Optimizes for SEO (meta tags, keywords, sitemap)
  7. 📊 Sets up analytics tracking
  8. 🚀 Deploys and gives you a shareable link

Step 3: Get Your Link

After generation completes, you get a shareable Vercel URL:

https://ergio.vercel.app/site/your-business-name

Share this link with anyone. It's live and ready.

Step 4: Sign Up to Save

Create an account to save your website, access the dashboard, and unlock all 21+ business engines.

Pro Tip: Try adding "3D" or "interactive" to your prompt for animated websites with Three.js. Example: "An interactive 3D portfolio for a Lagos-based photographer."

🎯 Finding Paying Clients (Lead Scanner)

AI-Powered

ERGIO's lead scanner is your #1 client acquisition tool. It finds real people who need your service.

How It Works:

  1. Go to Dashboard → Leads or use the API
  2. Enter your business type (e.g., "web design", "restaurant", "plumbing")
  3. Enter your city (e.g., "Lagos")
  4. ERGIO scans 70+ search engines for people asking for your service
  5. AI generates realistic leads with names, phone numbers, emails, and intent scores
  6. Each lead gets a score (0-100) based on buying intent

API Usage:

POST /api/leads { "businessType": "web design", "city": "Lagos" }

The API returns leads as a stream (SSE) — each lead arrives as it's found. You'll get 8+ leads per scan.

Pro Tip: Use specific business types for better leads. "Web design" works, but "WordPress web design" or "e-commerce web design" gets more targeted results.

💳 Accepting Payments Online

Paystack

ERGIO integrates with Paystack to accept payments from customers across Africa.

Accepting Payments:

  1. Go to Dashboard → Payments
  2. Your customers get a Paystack checkout link
  3. They can pay with cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, USSD, and mobile money
  4. Payments are verified automatically via Paystack webhook

API Usage:

POST /api/payments { "action": "initialize", "amount": 5000, "email": "customer@email.com" }

Returns a Paystack checkout URL you can redirect customers to.

❓ FAQ & Troubleshooting

How long does website generation take?

About 30-60 seconds. The AI goes through 8 steps: planning, branding, images, content, HTML, SEO, analytics, and deployment.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. ERGIO handles everything. Just describe your business and the AI does the rest.

Can I edit my website after it's generated?

Yes. Use the chat-based Website Refiner at /api/refine to make changes in natural language. Example: "Make the header blue and add a contact form."

How do leads work?

ERGIO scans 70+ search engines for people asking for your type of service. When search engines are unavailable, AI generates realistic leads with Nigerian names, phone numbers, and buying intent scores.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. ERGIO uses free AI (Pollinations) and free search (SearXNG) as fallbacks, so there's a zero-cost barrier to entry. Premium features require your own API keys for higher usage.

How do I accept payments?

Payments are powered by Paystack. Initialize a payment with POST /api/payments and redirect your customer to the returned checkout URL.

Can I use my own domain?

Not yet directly, but you can share the Vercel URL or embed the website using the preview iframe.

The website didn't generate — what happened?

If generation times out, try again with a shorter prompt. The AI might be busy. ERGIO has multiple AI fallbacks (Groq → OpenRouter → Pollinations) to ensure it always works.

How do I restart the interactive guide?

Click the "🎓 Guide" button in the bottom-left of the dashboard, or run ERGIO_GUIDE.reset() in the browser console.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026 · Governing Law: Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019

In plain English: We collect what we need to run ERGIO. We never sell your personal data. You own your business data. You can delete everything anytime.

1. Who Controls Your Data

ERGIO (Data Controller) — Lagos, Nigeria. Email: privacy@ergio.co
We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and strive to align with GDPR for our international users.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide, operate, and improve the ERGIO platform
  • Generate AI websites, brands, and content for you
  • Process subscription payments and send receipts
  • Send product updates, alerts, and support messages
  • Detect fraud and protect platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, regulatory)

We do NOT use your data to train AI models, sell to advertisers, or share with third parties for their marketing.

5. Your Customer Data (Most Important)

When your customers submit leads or forms on your ERGIO website, that data belongs to you. ERGIO is a data processor for your customer data — you are the data controller. You must:

  • Have a legal basis to collect your customers' data (consent is easiest)
  • Maintain your own privacy notice on your ERGIO website
  • Handle customer data requests (access, deletion) promptly

6. Cookies

ERGIO uses essential cookies (login sessions, preferences) and optional analytics cookies. You can manage cookies via the cookie banner on first visit. We use no advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages and API calls
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt — we never see them
  • Row-level security in Supabase — users only see their own data
  • API keys encrypted at rest in environment variables
  • Automatic security monitoring via Supabase

8. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data retained as long as account is active
  • Cancelled accounts: data retained 30 days then permanently deleted
  • Payment records: retained 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • Server logs: retained 90 days

9. Your Rights (NDPR + GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all your personal data
  • Correction — correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — export your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to data processing for specific purposes
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for optional processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@ergio.co. We respond within 72 hours.

10. Children's Privacy

ERGIO is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover a child has registered, we immediately delete their account.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States (Supabase, Vercel) and Ireland (various EU providers). All transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards including standard contractual clauses.

12. Changes to This Policy

We'll notify you by email 14 days before making material changes. The current version is always at ergio.vercel.app/ergio/privacy.html.

13. Contact & Complaints

Privacy questions: privacy@ergio.co
You may also file a complaint with Nigeria's National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) — the NDPR supervisory authority.

👤Who we are

ERGIO is an AI-powered business management platform built for African entrepreneurs. We are operated by ERGIO Technologies Ltd., registered in Nigeria. When you use ERGIO, we act as the data controller for your personal information.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@ergio.ng

📦What data we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account information — your name, email address, phone number, and business name when you sign up
  • Business data — your service descriptions, pricing, images, and content you add to your business profile
  • Booking & transaction data — client bookings, payment records, and invoice history (processed securely via Paystack)
  • Usage data — pages you visit, features you use, and actions you take within ERGIO (to improve the product)
  • Communication data — messages sent through the platform, WhatsApp bot interactions, and AI outreach emails

We also automatically collect:

  • Your IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Referring URLs and how you found ERGIO
  • Timestamps and session data

🎯How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your ERGIO account and business profile
  • Power the AI engines that find and connect you with real clients
  • Process payments and send you transaction records via Paystack
  • Send you booking notifications via WhatsApp and email
  • Improve ERGIO's features, AI models, and user experience
  • Send product updates and important account notices (you can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

🤝Who we share it with

We never sell your personal data. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers — Paystack (payments), Twilio (WhatsApp), Groq (AI), Supabase (database), and Google (email). All are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Your clients — your name, business name, and contact details are shared with clients you connect with through ERGIO
  • Legal requirements — if required by Nigerian law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users

🔐How we protect your data

We take security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Paystack (PCI DSS compliant) — we never see or store card numbers
  • Access to production data is restricted to essential personnel only
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

  • Objection — opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@ergio.ng and we will respond within 30 days.

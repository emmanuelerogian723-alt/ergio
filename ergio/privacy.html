Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Ergio ("we", "us", "our") is an AI Business Operating System that helps entrepreneurs in Africa build websites, find clients, and grow their businesses. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email, phone number, business information
  • Business data: Business name, type, services, location, pricing
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, website analytics
  • Cookies: Session cookies, authentication tokens, preferences

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To build and host your business website
  • To find potential clients through our lead scanner
  • To send you leads, bookings, and business notifications
  • To process payments through Paystack
  • To improve our AI models and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication, session management
  • Analytics: Understanding how you use Ergio (Google Analytics)
  • Functional: Remembering your preferences and settings

You can control cookies through our cookie consent banner or your browser settings.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Payment data is processed by Paystack and is never stored on our servers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase (database hosting, EU region)
  • Paystack (payment processing)
  • Vercel (website hosting)
  • Google (authentication, if you choose Google login)

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through your dashboard
  • Request data export or deletion
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw cookie consent at any time

Contact us at privacy@ergio.app for any data requests.

8. Lead Scanner

Our lead scanner searches public internet sources (search engines, directories) to find potential clients for your business. We only collect publicly available contact information and do not access private or restricted data.

9. Children's Privacy

Ergio is for entrepreneurs aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Ergio evolves. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

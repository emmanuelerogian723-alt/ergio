FAQ

Questions?

Is ERGIO a website builder?
No. Website builders make websites. ERGIO builds entire businesses — website, brand, payments, booking, client acquisition, marketing, operations. Then it runs everything 24/7 and goes out to find clients for you.
How does ERGIO find clients?
Four engines work together: Local Discovery puts you on Google Maps and directories. Demand Matching connects you with people actively searching for your service. AI Outreach scans the internet for businesses that need what you do and sends personalized emails. Repeat Clients keeps past clients coming back with automated follow-ups.
Do I need to know how to code?
Zero coding. You describe what you do in plain English. ERGIO handles everything — design, development, payments, SEO, marketing, and client acquisition. You just run your business.
What if I don't have money to start?
The Starter plan is free forever. You get a website, brand, and basic listing. When you start earning and want the client acquisition engines, upgrade to Pro. The first naira you make is yours — no commission on the free plan.
Which payment systems are supported?
Paystack and Flutterwave are built in. Your clients can pay by card, bank transfer, or USSD. Money goes straight to your account. ERGIO tracks every payment and sends invoices automatically.
Can ERGIO really run my business 24/7?
Yes. ERGIO's WhatsApp bot responds to clients, books appointments, sends follow-ups, and collects payments — even while you sleep. The AI outreach engine continues searching for new clients around the clock.
